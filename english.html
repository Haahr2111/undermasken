About us

Under Masken is a bar in the most centrally located basement in all of Aarhus.

We offer you a "hyggelig" athosphere, an open and homie environment and a lot of events. Even the strangest fish is welcome to visit us at the bar.

History

Under Masken first opened its doors in 1995.

The bar is located in the basement under Hotel Royal in Aarhus and the hotel holds a special place in the story of how Under Masken got started.

The creator and owner of Under Masken, Hans Krull, made the three iconic sculptures in front of the legendary Hotel Royal. As part of the payment he got the chance to lease the old staff bar in the basement of the hotel.

Since 1995 Hans Krull has decorated the place with artistic treasures from around the world and his own creations, made in his workshop on Bali.

Hans Krull is, in truth, an exciting person, and Under Masken reflects this with its whimsical expression and cozy, homely and relaxed atmosphere. Under Masken is the place for provincial cosmopolitans, professional amateurs and even the strangest fish feels welcome.

And we have plenty of fish. The back wall in the bar contains four big aquariums. The homepage of this website shows one of our beautiful aquariums.
